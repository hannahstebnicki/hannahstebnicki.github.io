Hi I'm Hannah!

I grew up in Poland near a peninsula called Hel ( I can say - "I've been to Hel and back many times" ). When I was fifteen years old I moved to the U.S. started High School with almost no prior education and a slight language barrier - seven years later I was the first person out of my family to graduate from college. How did I do it? Through sheer grit, diligence, work ethic, and determination to succeed. Now, I have become passionate about problem-solving because I know there is no limit to what you can achieve when you set your mind on success. And solving problems with the right people makes a world of a difference.
